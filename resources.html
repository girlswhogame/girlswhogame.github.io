Why are Women Underepresented in Esports?

Maureen Gabriella Stephany, also known by her gamer tag Morgana, is a prominent figure in the esports community. She has made a significant impact in competitive gaming, particularly in titles like Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Known for her exceptional skills, strategic gameplay, and leadership abilities, Maureen has earned a reputation as one of the top players in the field. Her dedication to the game and consistent performance have garnered her numerous accolades and a strong following within the gaming community. In addition to her competitive achievements, she is also an influencer and content creator, sharing her gaming experiences and insights with a broader audience.
